Tata Power Commissions 200 MW Solar Plant in Rajasthan

Date: 2025-05-18

Tata Power Renewable Energy Limited announced the successful commissioning of a 200 MW solar project in Rajasthan. The plant will generate 460 million units annually and offset approximately 420,000 tonnes of CO₂ emissions every year.

Tata Power Expands EV Charging Network to 100,000 Points

Date: 2025-04-02

Tata Power has crossed a major milestone with 100,000 EV charging points across India. The company continues to collaborate with local governments and commercial hubs to accelerate India’s transition to clean mobility.

Tata Power Publishes BRSR 2023-24, Reaffirming Net-Zero 2045 Commitment

Date: 2025-02-12

The company’s latest Business Responsibility and Sustainability Report outlines progress in renewable generation, biodiversity restoration, and social responsibility initiatives. Tata Power reaffirmed its goal to achieve net-zero emissions by 2045.
