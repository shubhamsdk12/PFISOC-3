Coal India Increases Renewable Investments

Date: 2024-11-14

Coal India Limited announced plans to invest ₹6,000 crore in solar power projects over the next three years. The initiative aligns with the company’s diversification strategy and India’s national renewable energy goals.

Coal India Reduces Operational Emissions by 7%

Date: 2024-08-27

In its 2023-24 sustainability update, Coal India reported a 7% reduction in operational CO₂ emissions compared to the previous year through improved energy efficiency and mine reclamation programs.

Coal India Launches Mine Reclamation Monitoring Portal

Date: 2024-06-10

The new online portal provides satellite-based transparency for mine reclamation activities across subsidiaries. The system enables real-time progress tracking for land restoration projects.
