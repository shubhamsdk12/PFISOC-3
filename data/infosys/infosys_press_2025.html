Infosys Achieves Carbon Neutrality Milestone in 2025

Date: 2025-06-04

Infosys today announced that it has achieved complete carbon neutrality across all global operations, becoming one of the first major IT services companies to meet this target ahead of its 2040 net-zero goal. The company achieved this through a combination of renewable energy procurement, on-site solar generation, and verified carbon offset projects.

Infosys Expands Renewable Energy Footprint

Date: 2025-04-22

On the occasion of Earth Day 2025, Infosys reported that 78% of its global electricity consumption now comes from renewable sources. The company plans to increase this to 90% by 2026 through additional Power Purchase Agreements and new rooftop solar installations across campuses in India and Europe.

Infosys Launches AI-based Sustainability Analytics Platform

Date: 2025-03-10

Infosys introduced a new AI-driven analytics platform designed to help enterprises track emissions, water usage, and supply chain sustainability metrics in real time. The platform is part of Infosys Cobalt, its flagship digital transformation suite.
